Workload Tracker Homepage

The Workload Tracker was originally an Excel spreadsheet, created with the intent of helping the Professional Affairs Committee (PAC) keep track of the amount of work each faculty member was doing as part of being on a committee, in order to try to distribute the responsibilities fairly. While the Excel sheet did an excellent job of keeping track of the information, it was difficult for a user to manipulate. Project PRONTO (PRoductive ONline TOols) approached this task with the intent of simplifying the election and record- keeping process, from gathering rotating-off positions to updating the list of committee members with the results from the elections.

Project PRONTO seeks to allow current students to build tools that they think are beneficial to Davidson College and to its community. To see any other projects they have worked on, please visit the Project PRONTO Website.
